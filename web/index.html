Rebuilding YouTube from the Bottom Up

To bypass the traditional recommendation system, we mapped YouTube from the bottom up by focusing on real human behavior. We profiled consistent, socially validated users and tracked how they migrate between channels.

By analyzing 8.6 billion comments, we built a community network where channels are connected solely by shared commenters.

Our Research Questions

  • User Behavior: Is commenting structured enough to build a meaningful network?
  • Organic Structure: What structures emerge when we connect channels through people, not algorithms?
  • Recommendation: Can we build a transparent recommendation engine based on human interaction?
